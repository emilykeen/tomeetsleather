Novemeber 3,2016

This week the Toe Meets Leather crew talk about why the playoff system is biased towards 1 loss teams from the SEC. We also discuss where all the conferences stand, and how Kentucky could still end up in the SEC championship game in a rematch with Alabama. We also cover all the teams chasing bowl games, including Ole Miss and Oregon (who saw that coming). Finally Andrew has a GT player to talk about in his Fish Smithson report and Tommy tells us why Wins and Losses mean nothing in his Useless facts segment. The season only gets better (or worse depending on your team) from here everyone!
